Strategic vision
First 90 days at Intersect

Intersect is at an inflection point — scaling aggressively while navigating a Google acquisition. The first 90 days aren't about making a mark. They're about listening, learning, and building the foundation to move fast without breaking things.

Days 1–30  ·  Listen & Audit
  • ›Meet every member of the People team 1:1 — understand their strengths, pain points, and what's working
  • ›Complete a full HR systems audit: data integrity, payroll accuracy, compliance posture, and vendor performance across US & Canada
  • ›Map the current HR tech stack — identify integration gaps, data quality issues, and quick wins
  • ›Review existing vendor contracts, open compliance items, and benefits program performance
  • ›Attend a Team Week — experience the culture firsthand and build relationships across the organization
Days 31–60  ·  Align & Prioritize
  • ›Present audit findings to Cate and senior leadership — translate gaps into a prioritized remediation roadmap with timelines and owners
  • ›Partner with the team to co-build the Q3/Q4 People Ops roadmap — ensure it's grounded in their input, not imposed from the top
  • ›Establish data governance standards and reporting cadences that give leadership reliable, real-time visibility into headcount and compliance
  • ›Assess Google acquisition readiness — identify HR documentation, policy, and data requirements that will need to be addressed pre-close
  • ›Initiate benefits and vendor contract review cycle for upcoming renewal negotiations
Days 61–90  ·  Build & Execute
  • ›Begin executing highest-priority roadmap items — quick wins that build team confidence and demonstrate momentum
  • ›Stand up or strengthen the immigration program management process — critical for a scaling tech company with global talent needs
  • ›Launch or refresh HR policy documentation — ensure all policies are acquisition-ready, regionally compliant, and employee-accessible
  • ›Deliver 90-day progress review to leadership with clear metrics, updated roadmap, and a 6-month outlook tied to company growth targets
  • ›Have earned the team's trust — not through authority, but through showing up, following through, and making their work better
The through-line: Every decision in the first 90 days is in service of one goal — building an HR Operations function that Intersect can trust completely as it scales toward and through the Google acquisition. Clean data. Solid compliance. A high-performing team. Infrastructure that doesn't slow the business down.
